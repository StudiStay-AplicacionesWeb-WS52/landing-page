Our services

Secure rooms

Our priority is your safety. All rooms are equipped with state-of-the-art electronic locks and surveillance cameras so you can rest in complete peace of mind

Strategic location

The locations of our spaces are unbeatable, just minutes from your university or institute. Say goodbye to the stress of traffic and get to your classes or exams quickly.

Always be connected

All rooms have internet connectivity (Wi-Fi and Ethernet), so you can do your work, homework and exams online without problems. You won't be left disconnected!

Reservation flexibility

We understand that university life can be unpredictable. Therefore, we offer flexibility in your reservations, whether you need a room for one night or a whole week during your exams, we are here to adapt to your needs.
